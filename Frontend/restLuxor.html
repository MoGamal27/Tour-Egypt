Metro Restaurant
About the place
(4.5)
Metro Restaurant is a beloved Cairo institution, serving delicious Egyptian and Middle Eastern cuisine since 1937. Known for its casual, friendly atmosphere and consistently good food, Metro offers a wide range of traditional dishes at reasonable prices. The restaurant's art deco interior has remained largely unchanged over the decades, giving it a charming retro vibe. Popular dishes include ful medames, taameya, and their famous stuffed pigeon.
