The Pyramids and Sphinx

The last remaining wonder of the ancient world, the Pyramids of Giza have stood for over 4,500 years as a testament to ancient Egyptian engineering...

  • Location: Giza Plateau, 20km southwest of Cairo
  • Built: Around 2580–2560 BCE (Fourth Dynasty)
  • Main Structures: Pyramid of Khufu (Great Pyramid), Pyramid of Khafre, Pyramid of Menkaure, Great Sphinx
  • Visiting Hours: 8:00 AM – 4:00 PM (winter), 7:00 AM – 7:00 PM (summer)
  • Ticket Price: 200 EGP (additional fees for entering pyramids)
Location

Cairo Tower

Standing at 187 meters, Cairo Tower offers the best panoramic views of the city...

  • Location: Zamalek district, Gezira Island
  • Height: 187 meters (614 ft)
  • Built: 1961 (completed in 1965)
  • Features: Observation deck (360° views), rotating restaurant
  • Visiting Hours: 9:00 AM – 12:00 AM
  • Ticket Price: 200 EGP for foreigners, 60 EGP for Egyptians
Location

Salah El-Din Citadel

This medieval Islamic fortification, built by Saladin in the 12th century...

  • Location: Mokattam Hill near central Cairo
  • Built: 1176–1183 CE
  • Main Attractions: Mosque of Muhammad Ali, Al-Nasir Muhammad Mosque, Military Museum
  • Visiting Hours: 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM daily
  • Ticket Price: 180 EGP for foreigners, 30 EGP for Egyptians
Location

Grand Egyptian Museum

The Grand Egyptian Museum, located near the Giza Pyramids, is set to be the world’s largest archaeological museum...

  • Location: Giza Plateau, near the Pyramids
  • Expected Opening: 2025
  • Size: 480,000 square meters
  • Highlights: Tutankhamun galleries, grand staircase with royal statues
  • Visiting Hours: To be announced
  • Ticket Price: To be announced
Location

Khan El Khalili Bazaar

Established in the 14th century, Khan El Khalili is Cairo’s most famous traditional market...

  • Location: Islamic Cairo district
  • Established: Late 14th century
  • Main Attractions: Souvenir shops, El Fishawy Café, traditional crafts
  • Visiting Hours: Most shops open 9:00 AM – 9:00 PM
  • Entry: Free
Location

Al-Azhar Mosque

Founded in 970 CE, Al-Azhar Mosque is one of the oldest and most prestigious Islamic institutions in the world...

  • Location: Islamic Cairo, near Khan El Khalili
  • Founded: 970 CE by the Fatimid Caliphate
  • Significance: Seat of Al-Azhar University
  • Architecture: Mix of Fatimid, Mamluk, and Ottoman styles
  • Visiting Hours: Open daily, except during prayer times
  • Entry: Free
